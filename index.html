Saimou (Simon) Zhang

Ph.D. student in Linguistics · University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign

I study language, writing, and cognition using both theoretical and computational approaches. My background spans applied linguistics, statistics, educational measurement, discourse analysis, and language teaching. I am especially interested in computational models of discourse, coherence in writing, AI-mediated writing support, and language technologies that better serve learners, writers, and educators.
