About

I study language, writing, and cognition using both theoretical and computational approaches. My background spans applied linguistics, statistics, educational measurement, and discourse analysis, which shapes the way I think about language data and human-centered technology.

I am especially interested in computational models of discourse, coherence in writing, AI-mediated writing support, and how language technologies can better serve learners, writers, and educators.
